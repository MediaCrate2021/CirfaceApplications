Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Data We Handle

When you use Migration Tool, the following data is temporarily processed:

  • Your identity: Your name and email from your Asana OAuth session.
  • Client data: Tasks, comments, attachments, users, and custom fields from the source platform and destination Asana workspace, as required to perform the migration you initiate.
  • API credentials: Source and destination API tokens you enter are held in your encrypted session only and are never written to disk or logged.

2. How Data Is Used

Client data is used solely to perform the migration you configure. It is not analysed, retained after your session ends, or shared with any third party beyond the platforms involved in the migration (source platform and Asana).

3. Data Retention

Session data (including credentials and migration state) is held in server memory for the duration of your session (up to 8 hours). No client data is persisted to a database.

4. Security

All connections are over HTTPS in production. Session cookies are httpOnly and secure. API tokens are never logged or exposed in client-side code.

5. Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring that client consent has been obtained before processing their data through this tool, and for handling all data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and Cirface's data policies.

6. Contact

Questions? Contact the internal tools team at Cirface.
